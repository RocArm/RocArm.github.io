Roc Armenter

Executive Vice President and Economist, FRB Philadelphia

Visiting Scholar, Wharton School - Finance

Areas of Expertise

Monetary Policy Macroeconomics Finance Machine Learning
rocarm@wharton.upenn.edu

I have more than two decades of experience in economic research and monetary policy at the Federal Reserve. I am most proud of my contributions to monetary policy over the years, as senior policy advisor and FOMC economist, and of my role in the design of balance sheet policies and implementation framework.

My research has been published in a variety of top journals in economics, with interests ranging from fiscal and monetary policy to information theory and machine learning.

I have a Ph.D. in economics from Northwestern University and an M.Sc. and a B.A. in economics from the Universitat Pompeu Fabra.
